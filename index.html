About Emma

Emma is the leading sleep tech company in Europe. The D2C company was founded in Germany in 2015. In merely 6 years, Emma sold over 4 million mattress and became the most awarded sleep brand in Europe.

In 2021, we achieved 750 million USD net revenue. Currently, we have 870+ employees and are present in 30+ countries worldwide. Emma has a wide range of products including mattresses, bed frames and sleep accessories across multiple price tiers.

At Emma, we have the constant drive to improve the way people sleep. With the help of patented technologies and the experience of our in-house team of sleep experts, we create the best sleep solutions.

Emma Sense

The heart of SENSE is the Automatic Contouring Technology, a dynamic system within the mattress. This mattress is equipped with 3 zones of smart chambers in the shoulder, lumbar and hip area of the mattress that simultaneously detects and measures your unique body profile and adjusts the air pressure to provide the sleeper the most optimum support and comfort. Further, your desired firmness level, zone support and sensitivity to your movements are fully customizable with the help of our MOTION app. Our SENSE mattress also features our exclusive Titanium Fiber with Graphite comfort layer to ensure not only luxurious sleep comfort but also sleep quality, optimizing deep sleep.

1 Automatic Contouring Technology
2 Customizable Multi-Zone Support
3 Deep Sleep Optimized Titanium Comfort Layer
